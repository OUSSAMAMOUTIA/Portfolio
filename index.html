About Me

I am a web developer from Agadir, Morocco. If you had asked me four years ago what I wanted to be in the future, I would have said a doctor or an engineer. Until I came across web development in my third year of university. I was excited to learn all the ins and outs of the field and I couldn’t wait to work on projects and unlock new challenges. A chance I was given during a two-month internship with NT DATA MOROCCO in Agadir. During that period, I participated in the development of a travel application backend using Spring and its user interface with Angular Material. I also had the opportunity to work with experienced engineers who offered me guidance and inspired me to constantly develop my skill set. The Tech universe is evolving on a daily basis, therefore, it’s my priority to stay up to date with the relevant technologies to web development and reinforce the core ones I have already learned. To accomplish that, I worked on several personal projects using different technologies for each one.
